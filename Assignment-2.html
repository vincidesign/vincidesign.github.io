Assignment-2: DIY Project

Project Brief

Holographic Product Display With Infinity Mirror (Sound Reactive)

Motivation

Sound and light are pure natural elements with the help of technology and design I want to create some visual, lighting output on sound reactive to get excited and fascinate the people towards the virtual reality.

Project idea

Built a low cost infinity visual product display for giving virtual reality experience of users by using simple science and technology. Lighting visual would be reacting on the sound/Music to create the infinity visual illusion And holographic projection to display infinity floating object in the center of visuals.

Project idea

Arduino Uno

Mini breadboard or PCB

WS2813 Digital 5050 RGB LED Strip - 144 LEDs (1 meter) 

Prototype wires 

USB A to B cable

Mean Well Switching Power Supply - 5V 10A

230V Power cable with plug 

Bluetooth HC-06 module RF transceiver Slave 4-PIN

Sound Detection Sensor Module 3-PIN

220 Ω Resistor

1000uF 16V Electrolytic Capacitor

Glass 25 x 25cm (3mm thick)

Tinted one-way mirror window film 30 x 30 cm

Mirror 25 x 25cm (3mm thick)

Black paint can/spray
